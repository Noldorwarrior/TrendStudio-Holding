# Промты для генерации изображений — Кинохолдинг Investor Deck

## Техническая спецификация (общая для всех)

- **Генератор:** Google Gemini (Imagen 3 / Gemini image generation)
- **Формат:** 16:9 (1920×1080 px)
- **Назначение:** Фоновые и декоративные изображения для вставки в интерактивную HTML-презентацию
- **Палитра:** Тёмная кинематографическая — основной фон `#060D1A` / `#0A1628`, золотые акценты `#D4A843` / `#F5C563`, допустимы приглушённые синие `#0F1D32` / `#152238`
- **Стиль:** Investor-grade, кинематографический, премиальный. Никаких стоковых клише — чистая визуальная атмосфера
- **Важно:** Изображения служат ФОНОМ под текст и инфографику, поэтому центральная область должна быть достаточно тёмной и незагруженной для читаемости контента. Основные визуальные элементы — по краям и углам

## Слайд 1 — Титульный / Инвестиционный тезис

### Изображение 1.1 — Полноэкранный фон «Кинематографическая атмосфера»

**Роль в HTML:** `background-image` для `.s1-bg`, полупрозрачный overlay поверх, заменяет текущий `radial-gradient`. Задаёт тон всей презентации.

**Промт для Gemini:**

```
Cinematic dark background for an investor presentation title slide. Ultra-wide 16:9 format.

Scene: A film production studio seen from above at an artistic angle — silhouettes of professional cinema cameras on dollies, lighting rigs with Fresnel lenses casting dramatic golden beams through atmospheric haze, film equipment barely visible in deep shadow. The scene is abstract and stylized, not a literal photograph.

Color palette: Deep navy-black background (#060D1A to #0A1628), with rich golden light rays (#D4A843, #F5C563) cutting diagonally across the frame from upper-left, creating volumetric god-rays through film-set haze. Subtle blue-steel reflections on metal equipment surfaces.

Composition: The center 60% of the frame must remain very dark and relatively empty (this is where title text will overlay). Visual interest concentrated in the corners and edges — camera silhouettes in bottom-left, light beams and lens flares in upper-left and upper-right, a faint golden bokeh trail along the bottom edge.

Mood: Premium, exclusive, cinematic grandeur. Like the opening frame of an awards ceremony. The feeling of standing on the threshold of something extraordinary.

Style: Photorealistic with artistic post-processing. Shallow depth of field, subtle film grain texture, anamorphic lens characteristics (horizontal streaks in highlights). No text, no logos, no people's faces visible.

Quality: Ultra-high resolution, 8K rendering quality, professional color grading.
```

### Изображение 1.2 — Декоративная текстура «Плёночная перфорация» (опционально)

**Роль в HTML:** Тонкий декоративный элемент по краям слайда, очень низкая opacity (5–10%).

**Промт для Gemini:**

```
Seamless abstract texture inspired by 35mm film strip perforation. Dark background (#060D1A).

A stylized, minimalist representation of cinema film strip edges running vertically along both sides of a 16:9 frame. The perforations are rendered as faint golden (#D4A843) geometric shapes — barely visible, ghostly, like a watermark. Between the perforations, extremely subtle light leaks in warm amber tones create a vintage premium feel.

The center of the image is completely black/transparent — only the leftmost 15% and rightmost 15% contain the film strip texture. Think of it as a vignette frame made of abstract film imagery.

Style: Minimalist, graphic, almost invisible. This is a texture overlay, not a hero image. Ultra-subtle, premium. No text, no logos.

Quality: High resolution, clean edges, suitable for overlay compositing at 5-10% opacity.
```

## Слайд 4 — Юнит-экономика и ключевые драйверы

### Изображение 4.1 — Фон «Четыре грани экосистемы»

**Роль в HTML:** Фон за четырьмя карточками направлений бизнеса. Визуально объединяет четыре разных бизнеса в единую экосистему.

**Промт для Gemini:**

```
Abstract visualization of a four-part business ecosystem for an investor presentation. Ultra-wide 16:9 dark format.

Scene: A dark space divided into four subtle quadrants by barely visible golden dividing lines meeting at the center. Each quadrant has its own distinct but harmonious atmospheric element, all sharing the same dark palette:

- Top-left quadrant (Cinema/Studio): Faint silhouette of a film clapperboard or camera lens, rendered as a ghostly golden outline dissolving into particles. A few horizontal anamorphic lens flares.

- Top-right quadrant (Blogger Agency): Abstract social/digital motif — interconnected circular nodes in a constellation pattern, suggesting a network of influencers. Nodes glow in warm gold.

- Bottom-left quadrant (Content Production): Geometric shapes suggesting video frames or editing timeline — rectangular golden outlines at various angles, stacked with slight parallax depth.

- Bottom-right quadrant (Distribution): Radiating lines spreading outward from center, like a distribution network or broadcast signal, in fading golden gradients.

At the center where all quadrants meet: a bright golden point of light — the unifying core of the holding company. Subtle golden energy lines flow from this center point outward into each quadrant.

Color palette: Ultra-dark navy-black background (#060D1A). All visual elements in gold (#D4A843, #F5C563) at very low opacity (15-25%). The quadrant dividing lines should be barely perceptible.

Composition: Everything must be very subtle and atmospheric. The four cards will overlay this, so the quadrant motifs should peek through at the edges and corners of each card position. Overall darkness level: 85-90% dark.

Mood: Sophisticated integration. Four distinct businesses, one unified vision. Synergy made visible.

Style: Abstract, geometric, minimalist. Like a constellation map of a business universe. No text, no logos, no photorealistic objects.

Quality: Ultra-high resolution, clean vector-like quality, precise geometric elements.
```

Style: Abstract, cinematic, atmospheric. Like a sci-fi corridor of destiny rendered in gold and black. Elegant simplicity. No text, no faces, no literal objects.

## Дополнительные элементы (универсальные)

### Изображение 7 — Декоративный разделитель между слайдами

**Роль в HTML:** Transition-элемент или тонкая линия-декорация (16:1 aspect ratio, горизонтальная полоска).

**Промт для Gemini:**

```
Ultra-wide horizontal decorative divider for a premium dark presentation. Aspect ratio 16:1 (e.g. 1920×120 px).

A thin horizontal golden light streak centered in a dark void. The streak starts as invisible at both edges, builds to a bright golden center (#F5C563), and fades again. Along the streak, tiny sparkle points create a "stardust" effect. Below and above the streak: pure darkness (#060D1A).

Think of it as a single golden thread stretched across a dark universe — minimal, elegant, precious.

Style: Minimalist, clean, premium. No text, no symbols. Just light and darkness.
```

### Изображение 8 — Абстрактная текстура «Золотой noise» для overlay

**Роль в HTML:** Тонкая шумовая текстура для наложения поверх градиентных фонов (opacity 3–5%) — добавляет тактильность и глубину.

**Промт для Gemini:**

```
Seamless subtle noise texture for a premium dark presentation overlay. Square format, tileable.

A fine grain noise pattern on a pure dark background (#060D1A). The noise particles are in very dark gold (#D4A843 at 8-10% opacity) — barely visible, like the texture of expensive matte paper or the grain of cinematic film stock.

The noise should be fine-grained (small particles), evenly distributed, and perfectly seamless/tileable. No patterns, no gradients, no visible structure — pure organic randomness.

This is a compositing texture that will be overlaid at 3-5% opacity. It should add depth and materiality without drawing attention to itself.

Style: Invisible design. Felt, not seen.
```
